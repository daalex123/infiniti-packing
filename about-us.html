ABOUT US

Innovative, reliable packaging for Sri Lanka’s garment industry and the global brands we support—from poly bags and labels through to shelf-ready presentation.

OUR STORY

Who we are

Infiniti Packaging Solutions (Pvt) Ltd was incorporated in 2015 and since then has grown up to a trusted supplier of high quality packaging solutions for the apparel industry in Sri Lanka. We specialize in providing innovative, reliable and cost effective packaging product range that support renowned global apparel brands.

With a strong commitment to quality, consistency and customer satisfaction, we work closely with our clients to understand their unique and comprehensive packaging requirements and deliver solutions that enhance presentation, protection, and brand value of the product. Our product range is designed to meet the evolving demands of the garment sector while maintaining high industry standards.

At Infiniti Packaging Solutions, we believe packaging is more than just a product and it is an essential part of a brand’s identity and customer experience. Through professionalism, timely service and continuous improvement, we aim to build long term partnerships and become a preferred packaging partner for the apparel industry.
